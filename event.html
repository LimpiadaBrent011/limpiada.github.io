Activities & Rcreation

Embark on a journey of a lifetime! Come experience the many different and wonderful experiences france has to offer. From music and festivals, art exhibits and galleries, to the most delectable food, sweets, and desserts in the world you are in for a good time.

The Louvre Museum

Welcome to the Louvre, where art comes to life! From the Mona Lisa to ancient Egyptian treasures, come and behold the many wonderful and extravagant art pieces from the most famous artists and painters in history.

Learn More


Festival of lights

Immerse yourself in a dazzling celebration of illumination as the city transforms with enchanting light displays. From historic landmarks to hidden alleyways, our tour will lead you through a brief, magical journey, exploring the radiant beauty and artistic brilliance that define Lyon's Festival of Lights.

Learn More


Eiffel Tower

Visit one of the most iconic landmarks in modern history. The crown jewel of Paris, France the one and only Eiffel tower. Come visit and enjoy the view from within the Eiffel tower.

Learn More


Ile Saint Louis

Cross the bridge from Notre Dame to Ile Saint Louis. There's often music playing, locals sitting along the banks of the Seine in the spring and summer, and it's perfect for people watching and enjoying the beautiful view. There's also a little wine shop where the owner, Herve, loves to give advice and tastings on French wine.

Learn More


French Cuisines and Cafe's foodtrip

France is hailed as one of the culinary centers of the world. Experience the wonderful delicacies, sweets, and unforgettable coffee and chocolate paired with the ambience of Paris, France what else can you want!

Learn More


Cruise the Canal du Midi

A canal cruise is probably the best option if you're searching for something "off the beaten track." The best way to explore France is by exploring its rivers. There are a tonne of activities and destinations to explore along the almost 3,000 miles of interior waterways and canals that can be cruised.
